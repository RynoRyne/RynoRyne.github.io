The Kitchen Enviroment: Fire, Sweat, and Sharp Objects

The professional kitchen is a hot, dangerous and stressful enviroment. Nearly every thing can burn or cut you including your fellow coworkers. It is also an enviroment that demands constant attention and a certain degree of perfection.




The stress of the enviroment is one of the reasons many people are drawn to the occupation of being a chef. While working as a chef you will definitly encounter the following:
  • extremely stressful situations
  • angry co workers
  • things that want to burn and cut you
  • over inflated egos
  • towel shortages
  • seriously they are like gold sometimes
  • friendships that last a lifetime
  • occasional moments of zen like flow state
